Spahgetti With Meat Sauce

Description

This quick and easy spahgetti with meat sauce is sure to scratch that italian itch you're having at 8:30 PM on a Thursday night with minimal ingredients in your pantry. With just a few simple ingredients and about 20 minutes of your time, you can be kicking back in your dining room enjoying some of the easiest and tastiest quick pasta you've had in your life.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • 1 lbs of ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Ground Beef seasoning
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, boil water and add pasta and a small amount of olive oil to keep it from boiling over as well as a dash of salt
  2. In a large saucepan, add ground beef, garlic, salt, pepper, and ground beef seasoning. Cook beef until full browned
  3. Strain ground beef and return to pan
  4. Add pasta sauce to ground beef, put heat to a simmer and stir until sauce has warmed up
  5. Strain pasta once al dente
  6. Combine pasta with meat sauce and enjoy
