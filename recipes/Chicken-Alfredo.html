Chicken-Alfredo

Description

This is a chicken alredo recipe that is guaranteed to make your mouth water and definetley put you on the toilet if you're lactose intolerant. While this recipe may be a bit labor intensive for all of you quick dinner kind of people, I can assure you that the work you put into this is WELL worth it.

Ingredients
  • Chicken Breast
  • Parmassean Cheese
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • One Box of Linguine
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Garlic Powder

Steps
  1. Cut chicken into small bite size chunks and season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  2. Cook chicken in a skillet until thoroughly cook through
  3. Shred block of parmassean cheese
  4. In a small pot, mix shredded cheese, garlic, 1 stick of butter, salt, and pepper. Mix until a sauce like consistency is reached
  5. In a large pot, boil and salt water, then add pasta
  6. Cook pasta until al dente
  7. Once pasta is finished cooking, add pasta, sauce, and chicken together on a plate. Eat and enjoy
