French Toast

Description

This recipe is sure to be the one thing that actually makes you want to get up in the morning. I mean who doesn't want to wake up and shove their mouth full of that sweet, delicous, thick, creamy, french toast. This recipe is luckily super easy to make, which is nice for those of us who want to put in minimal effort when we wake up in the morning to feed ourselves. All we need is 4 ingredients, a little morning will power and we're good to go.

Ingredients
  • 4 Slices of Bread
  • 1 Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Steps
  1. Crack the egg in a bowl
  2. Add the vanilla extract
  3. Add the 1/4 cup of milk
  4. Add the 1/2 Teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  5. Whisk all ingredients together and then start preheating a medium sized pan
  6. Take a slice of bread and evenly coat it with the mixture
  7. Cook bread on pan until golden brown on each side
